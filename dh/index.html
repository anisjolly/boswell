About

I started drawing comic strips after an email conversation prompted me to draw the first cartoon here, Spider. After I'd finished it, I realised that I had many more terrible ideas so I started drawing them and created this site. 20 years ago, my art teacher told me I was one of the worst pupils he'd ever had, but I proved him wrong. Kind of.

Want to contact me?

Perhaps you'd like to tell me how much you love my humour. Or send me some hate mail. Maybe you'd like to offer me lucrative printing partnership or you'd just like me to organise a casino heist for you.
